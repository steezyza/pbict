Our Mission & Purpose

Our mission is to champion radical economic transformation in South Africa’s STEAMIE sectors (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship). We advocate for the meaningful participation and ownership of Black people and SMMEs, ensuring they are not just participants but key drivers of the digital economy.

Our Principles

The Core Pillars of Our Ideology

Our Shared Purpose

We are united by a common goal: to build a truly inclusive and equitable ICT sector that reflects the diversity of South Africa and empowers its people.

Universal Access

We advocate for policies that guarantee unrestricted market entry, ensuring that every individual, regardless of background, has the opportunity to participate.

Ending Monopolization

We challenge monopolistic practices by fostering a distributed economic system that supports startups, SMMEs, and diverse business models to drive innovation.

Our Collaborative Approach

We believe in the power of partnership, uniting government, private enterprise, and civil society to create sustainable and impactful solutions for all.

Democratic and Inclusive

Our organization is member-driven and community-focused, ensuring that our strategies and actions are guided by the voices and needs of the people we serve.

“We envision a South Africa where opportunities are shared equitably, and technology empowers all.”

Our Programmes

#AccessAndOwnership

Ensures that Black people have full access and ownership in the STEAMIE sector, creating opportunities for business growth, and employability.

#Internet4All

Focuses on bridging the digital divide by ensuring all communities, regardless of location or socioeconomic status, are connected and can benefit from the digital economy.

#GateKeepersMustFall

Aims to challenge systems, policies, organisations, and structures that promote monopolization for the few in the sector, ensuring equal opportunities for all.

#4IRSTEAMIE

Promotes awareness and adoption of digital skills such as coding, robotics, AI, and IoT to improve the way of life.

#LocalisMoja

Encourages the development and manufacturing of locally designed technologies and content, fostering homegrown innovation and production.
